BIRCH JUICE

Round Lab is a young Korean brand, on the market since 2017, immediately gained popularity and is the winner of many nominations in the beauty industry in South Korea. The main idea of ​​the brand is to create effective products to eliminate specific skin problems,
maintaining youth and natural radiance of the skin as long as possible.


The creators of the Round Lab brand use fresh and high-quality raw materials that are collected directly in the territory of
South Korea. For example, birch sap is extracted in the province of Inje, wormwood is from the sea coast of the island of Komundo, and black soybeans are from the county of Jeongseon.


The brand's assortment includes two lines: BIRCH JUICE and 1025 DOKDO.


The BIRCH JUICE line is based on birch sap, which has antibacterial properties, prevents the development of acne, soothes inflammation and normalizes the secretion of the sebaceous glands and gives the skin smoothness and radiance.
